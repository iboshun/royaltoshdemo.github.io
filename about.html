ABOUT US

we are one of the foremost Nigeria’s leading Real Estate Development Company. The Company was duly registered under Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) on the 27th day of September, 2005. Since inception the Company had executed several Joint Venture Projects in affiliation with CIVBUILD NIGERIA LIMITED. Our core services cut across Project Development, Joint Venture Partnership, Estate Management, Facilities Management, Property Consultancy. We are fully integrated real estate and property development company providing an array of real estate services to the satisfaction of our valued clients. We have excited several past and ongoing successful projects within and around Nigeria.
We have established our reputation in the real estate industry by demonstrating our ability to deliver projects to specifications, time lines and budget against all odds. Our business model is built on “Joint Venture Partnership and Built Operate Transfer Brokerages” with a reputation for delivery and fairness in dealing with property owners. We have grown our business using this model thus reducing the initial capital commitment required for land purchase.
